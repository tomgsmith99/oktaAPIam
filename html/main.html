In this scenario, one user (Carl Sagan) is subscribed to the "silver" level of access, which means he will be able to access the /planets endpoint with his access token by virtue of the "http://myapp.com/scp/silver" scope. Okta will mint the access token and include the "http://myapp.com/scp/silver" scope because Carl belongs to the "silverSubscribers" group in Okta.

Similarly, another user (Jodi Foster) is subscribed to the "gold" level of access, which means she will be able to access the /moons endpoint, and she will also be able to access the /planets endpoint by virtue of the scopes included in her access token.

Try clicking on the buttons as an unauthenticated user, and then as Carl and Jodi to get a sense of how the access tokens work with the API endpoints.
